Why We Built This

close

No budget for support staff — We couldn't hire dedicated support across multiple platforms (Zalo, Facebook, Discord).

close

12-hour timezone gap — Operating across Vietnam and US time zones meant messages sat unanswered for hours.

close

Zalo OA was critical — Vietnamese parents (key decision-makers) expected us to be present on Zalo, but we couldn't staff it 24/7.

close

No sales team yet — Needed AI to qualify leads and handle FAQs until humans could follow up.

Why RAG Architecture

science

Research-Backed Choice

Research showed RAG was the best approach for chatbots that need accurate, up-to-date information without constant retraining.

verified

Hard-Coded Critical Info

Pricing, refund policy, course details are hard-coded/prompted to ensure accuracy—we couldn't risk AI hallucinating on high-stakes questions.

Vector Search for Long Tail

Vector search handles the long tail of general questions without manual rule-writing for every edge case.

How It Works

n8n workflow: Intent classification → RAG retrieval → Sales AI response → Escalation detection

Messages flow through intent classification, context retrieval via RAG, and a sales-trained AI that tracks conversation stages. The system continuously monitors for escalation triggers.

Why These 7 Escalation Triggers

lightbulb

Each trigger was chosen based on research into where AI chatbots typically fail or frustrate users. The system is designed to let AI handle volume while protecting high-value or high-risk interactions for humans.

Escalation workflow: Trigger detection → Discord thread creation → Agent routing

Trigger Why This Matters Priority
B2B/School Inquiry B2B deals need human nuance and relationship-building Urgent
Explicit Human Request Respect user autonomy—if they ask for a human, they get one High
Refund Request Legal/policy implications AI shouldn't decide High
Negative Sentiment Frustration can escalate if mishandled by AI High
Qualified Lead Closing Human touch closes deals; AI qualifies them High
Loop Detection User stuck in repetitive answers needs real help Medium
Low AI Confidence Better to admit limits than give bad answers Low
