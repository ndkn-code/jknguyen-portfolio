Why I Built This

close

Gut-feel product decisions. Leadership was making choices based on intuition from traditional SAT prep, but we were an AI-first platform serving a different market.

close

lightbulb

What We Learned

Key discoveries from the data that changed how we built and marketed the product:

emoji_objects

Brain Teaser Was #1

"Question of the Day" had highest engagement. Vietnamese students preferred short learning sessions, contradicting our assumption that they wanted intensive sessions like traditional prep.

bug_report

Session Error Log Was Broken

Analytics revealed the error tracking feature wasn't being used. Investigation showed UX issues preventing students from logging mistakes, so we fixed it.

calendar_today

Weekend + Monday Pattern

Data showed peak usage on Sat/Sun/Monday. We shifted our best social media content and group notifications to those days, increasing engagement.
